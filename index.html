Murder Mystery with Detective Todder (Quiz Game)

Players will be tasked with solving 5 murder mysteries presented by Detective Todder. Players must read each passage and answer one question per passage. Each question will have 4 possible answers and the player must choose correctly to earn the most amount of points. Players will only have 3 tries per passage, before it moves on to the next story.
