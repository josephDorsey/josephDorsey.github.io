Players will be tasked with solving five murder mysteries with Detective Todder. To progress through this game players must read and then successfully solve one question per passage.

All questions for each passage have four possible answers and the player must choose correctly to earn the most amount of points. Players will only have three tries per passage, before it moves on to the next story.

Points System

Each incorrect answer is a -2 to the potential points they could get on this passage. The points system works like this:

  1. If the player gets the answer correct on their first try: 5 points
  2. If the player gets the answer correct on their second try: 3 points
  3. If the player gets the answer correct on their third try: 1 point
  4. If the player get none of the answers correct on their third try: the passage will become unresponsive and switch to the next passage.
